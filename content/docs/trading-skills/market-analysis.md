# Market Analysis Skills

## /market-top-detector

Detect market top formation probability using O'Neil/Minervini/Monty frameworks.

- **Time Horizon**: 2–8 weeks (tactical timing)
- **Score**: 0–100 composite
- **Methodology**: 6-dimensional evaluation
  - Distribution day count (25%)
  - Leading stock health (20%)
  - Defensive sector rotation (15%)
  - Market breadth divergence (15%)
  - Index technical status (15%)
  - Sentiment & speculation (10%)
- **Risk Zones**: Green (0–20), Yellow (21–40), Orange (41–60), Red (61–80), Critical (81–100)

## /macro-regime-detector

Detect structural macro regime transitions over 1–2 year horizons.

- **Components**: Market concentration (RSP/SPY), yield curve, credit conditions, size factor, equity-bond relationship, sector rotation
- **Regimes**: Concentration, Broadening, Contraction, Inflationary, Transitional
- **Requires**: `FMP_API_KEY`
- **API Calls**: ~10 (free tier sufficient)

## /market-environment-analysis

Assess overall market environment and stage.

- **Stages**: Risk-On, Base, Caution, Stress
- **Metrics**: Risk appetite, market phase, volatility state

## /us-market-bubble-detector

Detect market bubble formation using Minsky/Kindleberger frameworks.

- **Time Horizon**: Months to years (structural)
- **Target**: Identify 30%+ bubble burst risks
- **Score**: 0–15 scale
